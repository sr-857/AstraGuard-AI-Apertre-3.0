# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## üõ°Ô∏è Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders of the **AstraGuard-AI** community pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## üéØ Our Standards

### ‚úÖ Examples of Behavior that Contributes to a Positive Environment

**Professional Conduct:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and project
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Technical Excellence:**
- Providing detailed, actionable code reviews
- Sharing knowledge through comprehensive documentation
- Helping newcomers understand complex security concepts
- Acknowledging contributions from all skill levels
- Encouraging experimentation and learning

**Collaborative Spirit:**
- Collaborating openly with team members
- Giving credit where credit is due
- Supporting fellow contributors during challenges
- Celebrating community achievements
- Fostering an environment of continuous learning

### ‚ùå Examples of Unacceptable Behavior

**Harassment & Discrimination:**
- The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Doxxing: Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following

**Unprofessional Conduct:**
- Sustained disruption of discussions or events
- Dismissing or attacking someone for their level of experience
- Gatekeeping: Making others feel unwelcome based on their background
- Spamming issues, pull requests, or discussions
- Plagiarism or taking credit for others' work

**Security Violations:**
- Sharing sensitive security vulnerabilities publicly without proper disclosure
- Using project resources for malicious purposes
- Attempting to exploit vulnerabilities in project infrastructure
- Distributing malware or malicious code
- Violating responsible disclosure practices

**Other Conduct:**
- Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above behaviors

## üîê Responsible Security Disclosure

As a cybersecurity-focused project, we take security seriously:

### Reporting Security Vulnerabilities

**DO:**
- Report vulnerabilities privately to: **subhajitroy857@gmail.com**
- Provide detailed reproduction steps
- Allow reasonable time (90 days) for patching
- Work collaboratively on remediation

**DON'T:**
- Publicly disclose vulnerabilities before patches are available
- Use vulnerabilities for personal gain
- Attack or exploit project infrastructure
- Share vulnerability details with third parties without permission

### Recognition

We maintain a **SECURITY.md** file acknowledging responsible security researchers who help improve AstraGuard-AI's security posture.

## üìú Enforcement Guidelines

Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining the consequences for any action deemed in violation of this Code of Conduct:

### 1. üü° **Correction**

**Community Impact:** Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed unprofessional or unwelcome.

**Consequence:** A private, written warning from community leaders, providing clarity around the nature of the violation and an explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. üü† **Warning**

**Community Impact:** A violation through a single incident or series of actions.

**Consequence:** A warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with the people involved, including unsolicited interaction with those enforcing the Code of Conduct, for a specified period of time. This includes avoiding interactions in community spaces as well as external channels like social media. Violating these terms may lead to a temporary or permanent ban.

### 3. üî¥ **Temporary Ban**

**Community Impact:** A serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior.

**Consequence:** A temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public communication with the community for a specified period of time. No public or private interaction with the people involved, including unsolicited interaction with those enforcing the Code of Conduct, is allowed during this period. Violating these terms may lead to a permanent ban.

### 4. ‚õî **Permanent Ban**

**Community Impact:** Demonstrating a pattern of violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior, harassment of an individual, or aggression toward or disparagement of classes of individuals.

**Consequence:** A permanent ban from any sort of public interaction within the community.
